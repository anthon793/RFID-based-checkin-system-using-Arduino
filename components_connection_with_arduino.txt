Wiring Information
1) RFID MFRC522
Pin	    Wiring to Arduino Uno
SDA	    Digital 10
SCK	    Digital 13
MOSI	Digital 11
MISO	Digital 12
IRQ	    Unconnected
GND	    GND
RST	    Digital 9
3.3V	3.3V
Caution: You must power this device with 3.3V!

2) I2C LCD Module
Pin	Wiring to Arduino Uno
GND	GND
VCC	5V
SDA	A4
SCL	A5

3) Servo Motor
Pin	    Wiring to Arduino Uno
GND	    GND
VCC	    5V
Signal	Digital 6

4) Buzzer
Pin	     Wiring to Arduino Uno
GND	     GND
VCC	     5V
Signal	 Digital 5

5) LEDs
LED	                Wiring to Arduino Uno
Green LED GND	    GND
Green LED Signal	Digital 7
Red LED GND	        GND
Red LED Signal	    Digital 4
